Sejong University is a private university located in Seoul, South Korea. The university was founded in 1940 as Kyung Sung Humanities Institute. It was renamed Sejong University in honor of King Sejong the Great. Sejong University is well known for its programs in hospitality and tourism management. The campus is located near Children's Grand Park in eastern Seoul. Many international students study at Sejong University every year. The university offers undergraduate and graduate degree programs. Sejong University has research centers focused on science and technology. The school is also recognized for its work in artificial intelligence research. Students at Sejong University can participate in various clubs and activities. The university library provides study spaces and digital resources for students. Sejong University collaborates with institutions and companies around the world.
